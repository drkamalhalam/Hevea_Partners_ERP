PARTNER FINANCIAL LEDGER & DISTRIBUTABLE BALANCE ENGINE
ARCHITECTURE DESIGN WAVE

IMPORTANT:
This is an architecture and implementation design wave.

Do NOT modify:
- Ownership engine
- Ownership attribution guard
- Ownership calculation formulas
- Contribution engine formulas
- Reimbursement formulas
- Entitlement creation formulas
- Sale execution workflows already implemented
- Inventory logic
- Inheritance logic
- Numeric Precision Foundation implementation
- Existing OTP architecture
- Existing assignment architecture

====================================================
OBJECTIVE
====================================================

Implement a complete Partner Financial Ledger system that tracks:

1. Gross Revenue
2. Cost Allocation
3. Financial Adjustments
4. Distributable Balance
5. Distribution Payments
6. Partner Financial Position

Core formula:

Distributable Balance =
Revenue Credits
- Cost Debits
+ Adjustment Credits
- Adjustment Debits
- Distribution Payments

Negative balances must be allowed.

====================================================
ENTITLEMENT SOURCE OF REVENUE
====================================================

Revenue originates ONLY when entitlement rubber is sold.

Entitlement creation remains unchanged.

----------------------------------------------------
CONTRIBUTION MODEL
----------------------------------------------------

After Store Entry correction window closes:

Sheet KG entitlement distributed according to project ownership.

Scrap KG entitlement distributed according to project ownership.

----------------------------------------------------
50% REVENUE MODEL
----------------------------------------------------

After Store Entry correction window closes:

50% of Sheet KG entitlement belongs to landowner pool.

50% of Scrap KG entitlement belongs to landowner pool.

Remaining 50% distributed among economic participants only.

Landowner participates in economic participant pool only for contributions beyond recorded land notional value.

====================================================
SALE REVENUE RECOGNITION
====================================================

Revenue recognition occurs automatically whenever entitlement ownership is consumed by a completed sale.

Applicable sale types:

- Individual Partner Sale
- Store Sale
- Internal Partner Purchase
- Admin Override Sale
- Developer Override Sale
- Future Sale Mechanisms

CRITICAL RULE:

Revenue attribution must always follow entitlement ownership consumption.

Revenue attribution must NEVER follow:

- Project ownership percentage
- Sale executor identity
- Current stock percentage
- Current ownership percentage

The system must identify whose entitlement was consumed and credit revenue accordingly.

====================================================
INDIVIDUAL PARTNER SALE
====================================================

When Partner A sells:

- Partner A entitlement decreases.
- Partner A gross revenue increases by sale proceeds.
- Inventory reduction logic remains unchanged.

====================================================
STORE SALE
====================================================

Store sale allocation logic remains unchanged.

Revenue allocation must follow actual entitlement quantities consumed from participating eligible partners.

Revenue must NOT be allocated according to project ownership.

Revenue must NOT be allocated according to stock percentages.

Revenue must follow actual entitlement consumption used in sale allocation.

Each participating partner receives revenue only for entitlement consumed from their own balance.

====================================================
INTERNAL PARTNER PURCHASE
====================================================

Approved business rule:

Seller:
- Entitlement decreases.
- Gross revenue increases by negotiated sale value.

Buyer:
- Entitlement increases.
- Gross revenue unchanged.

Buyer payment occurs externally.

No project revenue created.

No project ownership transfer.

No inventory movement.

Same physical stock remains in same store/place.

Only entitlement ownership changes.

====================================================
ADMIN / DEVELOPER OVERRIDE SALES
====================================================

Existing override sale rights remain unchanged.

Admin or Developer may execute sale involving:

- blocked entitlement
- disabled entitlement
- otherwise restricted entitlement

Override reason mandatory.

Override fully auditable.

IMPORTANT:

Admin or Developer never becomes revenue owner.

Revenue still belongs to the entitlement owners whose entitlement was consumed.

Sale executor identity and revenue ownership must remain separate concepts.

====================================================
COST ALLOCATION
====================================================

----------------------------------------------------
CONTRIBUTION MODEL
----------------------------------------------------

Costs allocated according to project ownership percentages.

----------------------------------------------------
50% REVENUE MODEL
----------------------------------------------------

Costs allocated entirely to landowner side.

Economic participant revenue remains cost free.

Make this rule explicit throughout the financial architecture.

====================================================
PARTNER FINANCIAL LEDGER
====================================================

Design an append-only ledger supporting:

Revenue Credit
Cost Allocation Debit
Adjustment Credit
Adjustment Debit
Distribution Payment Debit
Manual Override Credit
Manual Override Debit
Sale Reversal Credit
Sale Reversal Debit
Inheritance Transfer Out
Inheritance Transfer In

Every ledger record must contain:

Project
Partner
Date
Amount
Entry Type
Reference Type
Reference ID
Created By
Reason
Timestamp

No hard delete.

No editing of historical financial records.

Correction must occur through reversal entries.

====================================================
DISTRIBUTABLE BALANCE
====================================================

For each partner:

Distributable Balance =
Revenue Credits
- Cost Debits
+ Adjustment Credits
- Adjustment Debits
- Distribution Payments

Negative balances allowed.

Negative balances must remain visible.

System must never silently reset negatives to zero.

====================================================
DISTRIBUTION PAYMENT WORKFLOW
====================================================

Admin or Developer may distribute money.

Workflow:

Select Project
Select Partner
Enter Amount
Optional Proof Upload
Optional OTP Confirmation
Mark Paid

Creates:

Distribution Payment ledger entry.

Distributable Balance reduces immediately.

Partial distributions supported.

Over-distribution only through override workflow with:

- mandatory reason
- warning acknowledgement
- permanent audit trail

====================================================
PARTNER DASHBOARD
====================================================

Partner must see:

OWN DATA

- Own Sheet Entitlement
- Own Scrap Entitlement
- Own Gross Revenue
- Own Distributable Balance

PROJECT DATA

- Total Project Entitlement
- Total Project Sales
- Total Project Profit

====================================================
PROJECT PROFIT
====================================================

Project Profit =
Total Revenue
- Total Cost

Partner distributable balances may differ from ownership percentages because revenue follows entitlement consumption.

====================================================
SALE CANCELLATION
====================================================

Existing sale cancellation rules remain unchanged.

If:

Approved
+
Paid
+
Not dispatched
+
Cancelled

Then:

- entitlement restored
- revenue recognition reversed
- related financial ledger entries reversed
- full audit trail maintained

Use reversal entries only.

====================================================
INHERITANCE
====================================================

Upon inheritance:

- entitlement ownership transfers according to inheritance workflow
- undistributed financial balances transfer according to inheritance workflow

Historical ownership and financial audit records remain preserved.

====================================================
PROJECT CLOSURE
====================================================

Existing closure rule remains unchanged.

Project may close when:

Physical inventory = 0

Even if entitlement books contain residual mismatch.

Mandatory explanation required.

Explanation must remain permanently auditable.
