PERSON REGISTRY REFINEMENT & SINGLE SOURCE OF TRUTH IMPLEMENTATION

IMPORTANT INSTRUCTIONS

This is an existing Plantation ERP.

The architecture audit identified an existing Person Registry implementation:

- person_master
- person_master_audit
- person_role_assignments
- claimants
- nominees
- project_participants
- project_witnesses

These assets are reusable.

DO NOT rebuild Person Registry from scratch.

FIRST analyze existing implementation.

Reuse compatible structures wherever possible.

Architecture requirements take precedence over current implementation.

--------------------------------------------------
OBJECTIVE
--------------------------------------------------

Refine Person Registry into the single source of truth for all individuals.

Every human being in the ERP must originate from Person Registry.

No duplicate person records should exist in other modules.
