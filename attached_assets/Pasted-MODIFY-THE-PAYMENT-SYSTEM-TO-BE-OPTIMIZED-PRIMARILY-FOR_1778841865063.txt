MODIFY THE PAYMENT SYSTEM TO BE OPTIMIZED PRIMARILY FOR INDIAN BUYERS AND INDIAN BUSINESS OPERATIONS.

====================================================
INDIA-FIRST PAYMENT DESIGN
====================================================

The entire payment experience should prioritize:
- UPI payments
- QR code payments
- mobile users
- low-friction payment flow
- fast payment confirmation
- simple language

Most buyers are expected to use:
- Google Pay
- PhonePe
- Paytm
- BHIM
- bank UPI apps

Design the UX primarily around Indian payment habits.

====================================================
PRIMARY PAYMENT METHODS
====================================================

Highest Priority:
1. UPI QR
2. UPI Intent
3. Razorpay UPI

Secondary:
4. Net Banking
5. Debit Card
6. Credit Card

Optional:
7. Wallets

Cash payment support must remain available.

====================================================
UPI-FIRST UX
====================================================

Buyer should ideally complete payment in:
- scan QR
- approve payment
- done

Avoid requiring:
- typing account numbers
- complicated checkout forms
- unnecessary registration

====================================================
QR PAYMENT REQUIREMENTS
====================================================

System should:
- generate dynamic UPI QR
- auto-fill payable amount
- include payment reference/order reference
- support all major Indian UPI apps

Display:
- QR code
- payable amount
- UPI ID
- order reference

Provide:
- "Open in UPI App" button for mobile devices

====================================================
RAZORPAY CONFIGURATION
====================================================

Use Razorpay primarily for:
- UPI
- QR
- payment links
- callback/webhook support

Prefer UPI checkout as default payment option.

====================================================
PAYMENT VERIFICATION LOGIC
====================================================

Even if UPI payment succeeds:
- manual verification is still required
- dispatch must remain blocked until approval

This is critical for:
- dispute prevention
- wrong payment handling
- stock protection

====================================================
INDIAN BUSINESS PRACTICALITY
====================================================

Design system assuming:
- unstable internet may happen
- users may not understand technical terms
- users prefer WhatsApp communication
- screenshots/payment proof uploads are common
- many users operate fully from mobile

====================================================
PAYMENT PROOF SUPPORT
====================================================

Allow buyer to upload:
- payment screenshot
- UTR number
- bank reference

Store proof securely for audit.

====================================================
MOBILE-FIRST DESIGN
====================================================

Payment screens must be:
- large buttons
- readable on small screens
- fast loading
- simple language
- minimal steps

====================================================
LANGUAGE STYLE
====================================================

Avoid complex banking terminology.

Use simple labels:
- Pay Now
- Payment Received
- Payment Confirmed
- Waiting for Verification
- Upload Payment Screenshot

====================================================
WHATSAPP SUPPORT
====================================================

Prepare architecture for future WhatsApp integration:
- payment link sharing
- QR sharing
- order notifications
- payment confirmation notifications

====================================================
IMPORTANT INDIAN PAYMENT EDGE CASES
====================================================

Handle:
- delayed UPI confirmations
- duplicate UPI payments
- partial payment mistakes
- wrong reference numbers
- offline screenshot claims
- payment success but callback failure

====================================================
SECURITY
====================================================

Implement:
- duplicate UTR detection
- duplicate transaction detection
- webhook signature verification
- manual approval controls
- refund approval workflow

====================================================
UI PRIORITY
====================================================

The payment system should feel:
- familiar to Indian users
- similar to common Indian payment apps
- easy even for non-technical rural users

UPI QR should always be the most visible payment option.

====================================================
FINAL IMPORTANT RULE
====================================================

Design the entire payment experience around:
"Indian mobile-first UPI payment behavior"

The system must work smoothly for real-world Indian rubber plantation business operations.
