====================================================
PARTNER REGISTRATION RULES
====================================================

For every new ownership-capable participant:

Require:

- person_master record exists
- partner record exists
- partner linked to person_master

Prevent creation of ownership-capable participant without these links.

====================================================
CONTRIBUTION OWNERSHIP VALIDATION
====================================================

Before contribution creation:

Validate:

- project exists
- contributor partner exists
- contributor active
- contributor linked to person_master

Reject ownership-affecting contribution creation if validation fails.

Provide clear error message.

====================================================
PARTNER HEALTH VALIDATION
====================================================

Create validation checks identifying:

1. partner without person_master
2. ownership contribution with null partnerId
3. inactive partner with ownership contribution
4. duplicate partner records linked to same person_master
5. project participant missing required ownership partner record
6. ownership snapshot containing partner id not found in registry
7. transfer referencing invalid partner
8. inheritance referencing invalid partner

Expose through existing data-health framework.

====================================================
OWNERSHIP ATTRIBUTION PROTECTION
====================================================

Prevent ownership-affecting contribution verification if:

- partnerId null
- linked partner inactive
- linked person registry record missing

Verification must fail with explicit reason.

====================================================
TRANSFER VALIDATION
====================================================

Before ownership transfer execution validate:

- transferor partner exists
- buyer partner exists
- both active
- both linked to person_master

Reject execution if validation fails.

====================================================
INHERITANCE VALIDATION
====================================================

Before inheritance allocation finalization validate:

- source partner exists
- claimant linkage valid
- destination ownership holder resolvable

Reject finalization if validation fails.
