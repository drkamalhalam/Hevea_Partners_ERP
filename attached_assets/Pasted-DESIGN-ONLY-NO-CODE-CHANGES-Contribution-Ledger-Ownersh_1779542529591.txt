Ownership Sources 

Ownership may arise only from:

Land Notional Contribution Financial Inputs Task Expenditures Imported Contributions Withdrawal Adjustments Reimbursement Adjustments 

No other source may directly affect ownership.

Show exactly how each source enters the ledger.

Economic Bearer Principle 

Ownership belongs to the current economic bearer.

Example:

Partner A pays ₹100,000 reimbursable expense.

Before reimbursement:

Partner A receives provisional contribution credit. 

If Partner B reimburses:

Contribution credit transfers from A to B. 

If reimbursement never occurs before maturity:

Contribution credit finalizes to A. 

Show lifecycle and data model.
